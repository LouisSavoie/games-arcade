Fri, May 27 2022 - Minecart!

I just finished the first game, Minecart!

When I first starting learning JavaScript I build a few small games to get a feel for the language. Making those games is what inspired me to build this arcade and Minecart was the first game I made so I though it was fitting to remake it as the first one in the arcade.

Minecart is a game of luck where you mine stone and ores of varying rarities and weights trying to perfectly fill up your minecart. If you overfill it, you lose! If you manage to fill it perfectly, your score is determined by the combined rarity of the ores you mined.

It's a great game to play anytime you have a couple minutes. When I first built it I found myself playing it constantly! It's so simple yet somehow addicting.

Anyway, I hope you enjoy it!
